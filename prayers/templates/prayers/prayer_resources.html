Suicide/Crisis
•Hope Line www.thehopeline.com 1-800-394-HOPE (4673) Primarily geared towards people under 26
•National Suicide Prevention Lifeline http://www.suicidepreventionlifeline.org/ 1-800-273-TALK (8255) Not specifically Christian; connects to local crisis centers
•Groundwire www.groundwire.net N/A Primarily geared toward teens; 24 hour chat
•International www.suicide.org Resource for identifying prevention resource in different countries
•Veteran’s Crisis Line www.veteranscrisisline.net 1-800-273-8255 PRESS 1 Text 838255 Confidential chat also available on the website. Help for active duty, veterans, & family/friends.
